## FAQ

**Does the free tier expire?**
No. The free tier is free forever with no time limit.

**What happens when I hit my log limit?**
New logs stop recording until you upgrade or the monthly limit resets. Your app keeps running — nothing breaks.

**Can I cancel Pro anytime?**
Yes. Cancel anytime from your dashboard settings. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

**Is there a trial for Pro?**
Sign up free and upgrade to Pro when you need it. No trial needed.
